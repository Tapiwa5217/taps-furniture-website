About us

In brief, we are a shop for custom home furniture in Lusaka.

A Bit More About Us

We are a shop primarily focused on delivering high quality furniture to our clients that complete the look of their home. Our goal is to turn your house into a well decorated and comfortable space.

Our team of craftsmen have been making furniture country wide since 2016 and are well experience in delivering furniture that matches our customers' requirements.

Our idea of a company came to life in March of 2022. My family just completed the building of our house and needed furniture to match the theme of each room. We were lucky to come across a team of 4 talented men that made our kitchen unit, living room chairs and full bedroom sets.

I decided to team up with these men to form Taps Furniture and share this experience with others that want to furnish their houses as well with quality fittings. I have seen a lot of compromised quality on household furniture and most of it is overpriced, so my team and I have dedicated ourselves to working to deliver high quality furniture that can fit the client's budget.

as a business, we make kitchen units, beds, headboards and wardrobes, kitchen units, Tv displays or Tv stands, sofas and tables for your living room. We make these things according to your vision.

If you're wondering why you would choose us, our reasons are simple. We prioritize the four things that clients want: we deliver our work on time, we provide our customers with high quality work, we aim to provide these services at a reasonable price and make the customer's experience light and hassle free. All we want to do is give you the best!

- Tapiwa Musinga, Managing Director
